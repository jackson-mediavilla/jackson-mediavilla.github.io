ABOUT THE PROJECT

For the final project in this class, I am proposing a new statistic for evaluating baseball players. The project includes an explanation of the statistic, a demonstration, an evaluation of the statistic compared to existing statistics, and a presentation.

The explanation of my proposed statistic can be found on the Explanation of Idea page of this website. I describe why I think my statistic is a good one and to which statistics it most closely relates. In short, my proposed statistic is called Base Accumulation by Player Ability and evaluates players on the hitting and baserunning factors over which the player has control.

The demonstration is in the form of an application showing how the statistic works and can be found on the Use the Tool page of this website.

On the Analysis page of this website, I evaluate my statistic by comparing it with other statistics. I present other statistics along with my own and include a correlation score.

For the presentation, I have produced a short video that describes my project. A link to the video can be found in this project's GitHub Repository.
